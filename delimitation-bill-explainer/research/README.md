## What's in the blog vs. what's here

The blog presents a curated, objective subset of this research — focused on what general audiences need to understand the bill. This research archive contains the full depth, including:

- Analysis that was too technical or speculative for the blog (constitutional deep dive)
- The complete clause-by-clause bill reference (blog only covers 6 of 8 clauses in detail)
- Raw fact-check findings with correction history
- Cross-validation data against external psephologists
- Alternative formula proposals from think tanks (the blog only implements two: pure population and weighted)
- Economic contribution vs. representation data (GDP/GST angle not used in blog)
- The "816 design" analysis — how the reported plan preserves male incumbents
- Historical US apportionment parallel
- The 2027 census timing question
