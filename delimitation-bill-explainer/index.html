Methodology

Allocation: Hamilton/largest-remainder method. State/UT pool split per bill's 815:35 ratio. Minimum 1 seat per entity.

Gain/Loss: New seats minus what a state would get if its current share were simply scaled up: new − round(current × total / 543)

Weighted formula: Blends population share with a development bonus rewarding lower population growth since 1971.
