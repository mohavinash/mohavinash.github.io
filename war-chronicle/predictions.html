Methodology

This page shows outputs from a 106-node Bayesian Network modeling the US-Iran war. The network ingests OSINT from 23 RSS/Atom feeds, maps evidence to observation nodes via LLM, and runs Junction Tree inference + Monte Carlo simulation (50 samples per run). Predictions are updated daily. The model is backtested against 11 historical crises with 91.9% forward accuracy.

Confidence tiers are derived from the probability margin between the top-1 and top-2 states: High (margin ≥ 0.36), Moderate (0.10–0.36), Low (< 0.10). All posteriors are computed with KL-Capacity C=1.3 and temperature T=0.25.
